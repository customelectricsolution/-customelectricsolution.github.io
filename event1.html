Have you ever wanted to build your own little toy- a toy that does something or other for you?
Perhaps the answer is no; in which case, would you like to understand the basics of how engineering gets done?
If the answer is still no, you can stop reading right here and search for something you find fun.

After having more than one person expressing a desire to better understand the fundamental tools of the engineering trade, I thought to offer such a learning opportunity. Here is the outline created thus far:

- Introduction to Engineering
- Fundamental Principles in Engineering- aka Units of Measurements
- Relationships between Units
- Significance of Units in an Engineering Task

TL;DR: majority of discussion will be on scientific units and introductory level algebra. The reason: if your units do not match, your solution is definitely wrong.
